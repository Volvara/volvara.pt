About Karina

True warm stories,
honest moments

Hi, I'm Karina — a lifestyle photographer based in Quarteira, Algarve, working across the Algarve and Lisbon. Originally from Ukraine, I moved here with one simple mission: to capture the real you.

I don't do stiff poses or forced smiles. I create an atmosphere where you forget there's a camera — and that's when the magic happens. Genuine laughter, tender glances, the way you hold each other.

Families, couples, portraits: every session is a story told in light, warmth and authentic emotion.

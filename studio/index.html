What the Studio is

Smaller than an agency. Slower on purpose.

Volvara Studio is where we take on projects that deserve more time than the five-day turnaround of our standard work. One-person practice, based in Portimão. No account managers, no producers, no handoffs. The person who reads your brief is the person who designs, writes, and builds the site.

We take on a limited number of Studio engagements each quarter. Each one is quoted individually. We decide together whether the project fits — not every site needs a studio, and that is fine.

The common thread in the work we like most: the client has a clear sense of what they want to say, but isn't sure how to say it online. We help.

How we work

A conversation,
then a site.

Four stages. Typically four to eight weeks from first conversation to live.

01
Conversation
We talk — by email, call, or in person if you're in the Algarve. No forms, no discovery decks. You tell us what the business is and what the site needs to do. We ask questions. We establish whether the project is a good fit for the Studio or better served by our standard work.
02
Proposal
A short written proposal — scope, timeline, investment, what's included. No fine print, no options to confuse. If the terms work for both of us, we begin.
03
Build
Writing, design, and code happen in parallel. You see progress every week, not at the end. Two rounds of revisions are standard. Changes in direction are expected — clarity emerges as the work takes shape.
04
Live
We deploy, configure domain and hosting, verify search engines, and run through final checks with you. Then the site is yours. Ongoing maintenance is available, monthly or ad-hoc.
Good work takes time. — Studio principle
What we believe

A few quiet principles.

Hand-coded.
No page builders, no bloated frameworks, no drag-and-drop. Every line is written deliberately. It's how we make sites that are fast, findable, and pleasant to maintain.
Built to last.
A site we deliver today should still work in 2030. That means no reliance on trendy stacks, no vendor lock-in, and sensible, boring infrastructure underneath the surface.
Respectfully slow.
Good work takes time. We don't promise delivery in days — we promise care. If you need a site tomorrow, our standard work is the right fit. The Studio is for when the deadline is less important than the result.
